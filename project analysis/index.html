Project description

Create a web app for a bakery/tea house to help the owners extend their business online.

The customer will be able to:

  • choose between bakery or tea house
  • see the opening hours/contact/about
  • see the menu
  • register/login
  • search for products

After being logged in the customer can:

  • add products to shopping cart
  • order products
  • pay online (required for order above €50) or pay in the bakery

The owner's requirements for the project

Main

  • clear division between bakery and tea house
  • menu and prices
  • list with allergens
  • view in dutch and english
  • promotions: holidays and events
  • slider for images and promotions
  • admin login
  • contact form
  • mobile friendly
  • Separated section for takeaways

Orders

  • Order must be placed a day at front before 15h, big orders need to be placed 3 days in advance
  • Adding to shopping cart
  • quantity
  • time of pickup
  • customer data (through login)
  • direct payment required for order above €50
  • email confirmation
